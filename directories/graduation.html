Commencement: A New Chapter Begins

By Erick Gaceta • 5 min read

Graduates celebrate their achievements at BSU-BC's Gymnasium

The Loo National High School graduation ceremony marked the culmination of years of hard work for the Class of 2022. The event, held at BSU-BC's Gymnasium, saw graduates receive their diplomas across various disciplines.

Ceremony Highlights

The ceremony began with a processional march at 8:00 AM, followed by the national anthem and invocation. The School Principal delivered an inspiring commencement address emphasizing resilience and lifelong learning.

Academic Achievements

Several students graduated with honors, including top academic performers. The STEM strand produced the most honor graduates this year.

"These six years challenged us academically but also shaped our character," said one graduate.

Student Reflections

"The late-night study sessions and group projects prepared us for the real world," the graduate continued.

Future Plans

Many graduates plan to pursue college degrees, while others have already secured employment. The school reported a high college acceptance rate for this year's graduates.

Alumni Advice

Senior faculty members encouraged graduates to maintain their school connections. "Your network will be invaluable as you navigate your future," advised one teacher.
